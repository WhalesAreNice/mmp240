List of Artists

Ai Wei Wei

Ai Wei Wei was born on the 28th of August, 1957 in Beijing. He is a Chinese Contemporary artist and activist. As a political activist, he has been highly and openly critical of the Chinese Government's stance on democracy and human rights.

Alma Thomas

Alma Thomas was born on September 22, 1891. She was an African-American Expressionist painter and art educator. She primarily worked in Washington D.C. and she was described as a force in the Washington Color School.

Click here to go to Alma Thomas' Page

Ana Mendieta

Ana Mendieta was born on September 8, 1985. She was a Cuban American that came to the US as a refugee in 1961. She is a performance artist, sculptor, painter and video artist, who is best known for her "Earth-Body" artwork.

Click here to go to Ana Mendieta's Page

Carrie Mae Weems

Carrie Mae Weems was born April 20, 1953. She is an American artist that works with text, fabric, audio, digital images and installtion video but is best known for her work in the field of photography.
